TheChallenger Tutorial (1/13)
Welcome To TheChallenger App!

Here's a simple tutorial that explains what is to do here. If You want to explore on Your own, just close the tutorial and it won't bother You again :]

Here you can create a video competition that people can participate in.
Add a description and select a category that describes your challenge the best. You can set the difficulty level if You wish.
Choose privacy ( public - for all, private - only for your friends )
Select a video that describes what's your challenge all about. Maybe You would like to do it yourself first ? ;)
Your challenge is ready to join! You will be notified if someone responded or joined.
Discover Challenges lets you join challenges of other people.
Search panel lets you be more specific when looking for challenges. Maybe you want to join Your friend's challenge ? Just search by users!
Click one of the challenge names to see the details.
Click the Join button. You are now participating in a challenge. Time to create a video response. Good luck!
That's a hall of fame. Check out the most popular challenges and best users.
A headquarters for Your challenges. They are all here.
Thanks for keeping up with this tutorial. Always push Your limits and get out of the safety zone. Have fun!
